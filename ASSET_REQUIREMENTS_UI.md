# UI & Icon Asset Requirements (High-Roller Edition)

The UI must feel like a luxury dashboard. Avoid "flat" design. Go for **Glassmorphism** (frosted glass) with **Gold Borders**.

## 1. Primary Action Button (The "Spin" Button)
This is the most important element. It must feel physical.

- **`btn_spin_idle.webp`**: A large, circular or rounded-rect button. Green or Gold. Looks like a physical arcade button with a glossy plastic cap.
- **`btn_spin_pressed.webp`**: The same button, but slightly darker and shifted down (to simulate being pressed).

## 4. Backgrounds & Panels
The atmosphere of the casino.

- **`bg_main.webp`**: **"VIP Lounge"**. A dark, out-of-focus background. Deep purples and blacks. Maybe subtle bokeh lights in the distance (like distant slot machines). *Crucial: Must not be distracting.*
- **`panel_glass.webp`**: A 9-slice image (or CSS equivalent) for dialogs. Dark semi-transparent black glass with a thin **Gold Border**.
